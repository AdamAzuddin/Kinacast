EP 3 | UM Selamat Ke? featuring ASP/PB Mohd Nor Rasyeed — UM Auxiliary Police OfficerIn Episode 3, we welcome you ASP/PB Mohd Nor Rasyeed, an auxiliary police officer in University Malaya.
